What is Fortnite?

Fortnite is a dynamic online game of survival where 100 players fight to be the last one standing. This game stands out with its unique blend of shooting, building, and exploring amidst a vibrant, ever-evolving game world.

Fortnite's continual updates and vibrant community make it a standout in the genre of battle royale games.
