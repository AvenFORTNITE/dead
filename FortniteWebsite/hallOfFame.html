Legends of Fortnite

Ninja (Tyler Blevins)

Ninja is arguably the most famous Fortnite player in the world. Rising to mainstream fame through streaming Fortnite on Twitch, he has set numerous streaming records, including the most-viewed stream by a single player. Ninja's influence extends beyond gaming, having appeared on various TV shows and in major publications, effectively becoming the face of Fortnite and gaming culture to the broader public. His strategic play and entertaining commentary helped elevate Fortnite to a global phenomenon.

Nick Eh 30 (Nick Amyoony)

Nick Eh 30 is renowned for his positive demeanor and highly skilled gameplay. Known for his fast building techniques and clean, strategic gameplay, Nick transitioned from editing YouTube videos to streaming on Twitch, where he rapidly grew his following. His family-friendly content and engaging personality make him a role model in the community. Nick's participation in high-profile tournaments and his regular interactions with fans have cemented his status as a pivotal figure in the Fortnite scene.

Myth (Ali Kabbani)

Myth was one of the first Fortnite streamers to rise to stardom, known initially for his exceptional building skills and tactical prowess. As TSM's first Fortnite player, Myth helped set the standard for professional Fortnite gameplay and streaming. He played a crucial role in shaping the early competitive Fortnite landscape and remains a popular figure in the streaming community due to his insightful game analysis and charismatic personality.

Daequan (Daequan Loco)

Daequan is best known for his aggressive playstyle, memorable catchphrases, and charismatic humor. A member of TSM alongside Myth, he quickly became one of the most watched Fortnite streamers. Daequan has won multiple Fortnite tournaments and is credited with popularizing certain playstyles and strategies, including the use of double pump shotguns. His impact extends through his open discussions on topics like mental health, which have endeared him further to the community.
