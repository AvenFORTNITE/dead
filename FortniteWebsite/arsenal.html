The Fortnite Vault Opens...

Take a look at a few of the popular guns throughout Fortnite.

Assault Rifle (SCAR)

Legendary and Epic versions known for high damage and accuracy.

Pump Shotgun

Preferred for close combat with high burst damage.

Bolt-Action Sniper Rifle

Valued for its long-range capabilities.

Tactical Shotgun

Faster fire rate than other shotguns, useful in close combat.

SMG (Submachine Gun)

Effective at close to medium range with a high rate of fire.

Heavy Assault Rifle

Known for higher damage per shot in the assault rifle category.

Infinity Blade

A mythic melee weapon that grants increased mobility and damage.

Drum Gun

A fan favorite due to its high capacity and rate of fire.

Boom Bow

Combines the mechanics of a bow with explosive damage.

Hand Cannon

Known for its high damage and ability to shoot through structures.

Thanos Gauntlet

Allows one player in the whole lobby to turn into Thanos, unleashing his power.

Lightsaber

Lightsaber from Star Wars. This gives the player increased mobility and defense from oncoming fire.

Fortnite Weapons Ranked

Here's a ranking of some of the most popular Fortnite weapons, from strongest to weakest based on their effectiveness in gameplay:

  1. Assault Rifle (SCAR)

    Legendary & Epic versions known for high damage and accuracy.

  2. Bolt-Action Sniper Rifle

    Valued for its long-range capabilities and high damage per shot.

  3. Pump Shotgun

    Preferred for close combat with high burst damage.

  4. Heavy Assault Rifle

    Known for higher damage per shot in the assault rifle category.

  5. Tactical Shotgun

    Faster fire rate than other shotguns, useful in close combat.

  6. SMG (Submachine Gun)

    Effective at close to medium range with a high rate of fire.
